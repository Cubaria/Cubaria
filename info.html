What is Cubaria?

Cubaria is a webcomic that follows the journey of a young Kaiet on a quest to save his planet.

One day, a young boy finds an inexplicably cut gem that had cried out for help. Said gem grants him a quest to save his dwindling planet from a bloodthirsty warlord. Follow the crusade of Cubey as he expels the six pillars that absorb life from the planet of Cubaria!
