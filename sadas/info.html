WELCOME TO THE END

Follow the tragedy of Cubaria as a young boy comes to grips with his unknown reality as the lone savior of his planet.

Cubaria is a massive mixed media project following the stance of a feeble orphan on a quest to become a hero. Cubey, the aforementioned orphan, is scouting out the ruins of his planet and finds a ruby red gem. The gem tasks him with the salvation of his home. The only question is, is he up for the task?
